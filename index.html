Intro

I'm Thulane a final‑year Mechanical Engineering student at Kun Shan University (Taiwan), with a strong background in Quantity Surveying and Project Management. I bridge the gap between technical design and cost‑effective execution: from CAD modelling and control systems to bill of quantities and contract administration. My final‑year project, a vision‑based autonomous baseball pitching robot, demonstrates real‑time target detection, holonomic mobility, and projectile modelling with aerodynamic drag compensation.

As I prepare to graduate, I'm seeking opportunities in smart manufacturing, industrial automation, or HVAC engineering consultancy or construction. Welcome to explore my key projects or check the Projects section where I upload my work files and images.
