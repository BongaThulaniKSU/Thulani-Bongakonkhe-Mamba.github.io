Intro

I’m Thulane — a dual-discipline professional bridging mechanical engineering and technical quantity surveying. My approach fuses technical precision with financial accountability, ensuring that every project is not only structurally and mechanically sound but also economically optimized. From concept design to final account, I bring clarity, innovation, and cost certainty to industrial and construction projects.

With a background in heavy machinery, HVAC, piping systems, and comprehensive cost planning, I help stakeholders avoid budget overruns while maintaining performance standards. My mission: deliver robust engineering solutions that respect timelines, budgets, and sustainability goals. Explore my portfolio of work to see how I turn complex challenges into measurable value.

Work

⚙️ Mechanical Engineer | DRA Global (Johannesburg) | 2021 – Present
- Lead mechanical design and specification for mineral processing plants, including conveyors, chutes, and dust suppression systems.
- Collaborate with multi-disciplinary teams to produce construction-ready drawings, equipment schedules, and technical reports.
- Perform value engineering studies – achieved 12% cost saving on a major materials handling upgrade without compromising throughput.
- Conduct site inspections, failure analysis, and root cause remediation for mechanical assets.

📐 Technical Quantity Surveyor | Measurisk Cost Consultants (Pretoria) | 2019 – 2021
- Prepared detailed Bills of Quantities (BOQ) for industrial MEP projects, ranging from R5M to R85M in contract value.
- Administered NEC3 Option C contracts, evaluated interim valuations, and managed variations, claims, and final accounts.
- Carried out cost estimation and risk analysis for mechanical installations (piping, HVAC, fire protection) using CCS Candy and WinQS.
- Advised project teams on cost-effective material alternatives, improving overall cost certainty by 18%.

🏭 Notable Project: Centurion Logistics Hub
Acted as dual engineering & commercial lead: designed warehouse ventilation layout while managing tender documentation and procurement schedules – resulted in a seamless integration between design intent and budget adherence, delivering the project 6% under approved budget.
